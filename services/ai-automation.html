AI & Automation

Conversational AI, workflow automation, and predictive analytics that reduce operating costs and eliminate repetitive manual work.

Every business has processes that are repetitive, rules-based, and expensive to staff around the clock — customer inquiries, appointment scheduling, data entry, lead qualification. Via Codos builds AI and automation systems that take on exactly that class of work, reliably and at a fraction of the cost of human staffing for the same volume.

Our flagship capability, the AI Call Colleague, is a natural-language voice agent that answers calls, books appointments, and captures leads twenty-four hours a day, handling unlimited concurrent calls without hold times. Beyond voice, we build chat-based support agents, document-processing pipelines, and predictive models that flag risk or opportunity before a human would notice it.

We are deliberately conservative about where full automation is appropriate. Every AI system we deploy includes clear escalation paths to a human, transparent logging, and guardrails against the kind of unsupervised errors that damage customer trust.

What's Included

  • 24/7 conversational AI for calls and chat
  • Natural language processing and intent recognition
  • Workflow and business-process automation
  • Predictive analytics and anomaly detection
  • Human-in-the-loop escalation and oversight
  • Integration with existing CRM, helpdesk, and scheduling tools
