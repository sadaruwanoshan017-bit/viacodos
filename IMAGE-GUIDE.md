# Via Codos — Media Guide
Every filename below goes in the **`/assets/`** folder. The site works without them
(styled placeholders show), and each one lights up automatically the moment you drop the file in.

## Brand art direction (applies to every image)
- **Palette:** Navy `#2F4156` · Teal `#567C8D` · Sky Blue `#C8D9E6` · Beige `#F5EFEB` · White — with a restrained gold accent `#C9A24B` (matches the logo)
- **Mood:** calm, premium, editorial. Soft directional light, shallow depth of field, no neon, no purple/pink AI gradients, no lens-flare clutter
- **People:** natural, candid, South Asian engineering teams where people appear — never stocky posed handshakes
- **Consistency:** shoot/generate everything as if it's one photo essay: same light temperature, same grade
